# Add The Kiwi Dialectic course calendar to your device

Kia ora,

The Kiwi Dialectic now has a public course calendar for readers who want the study schedule in one place.

Some courses are open to everyone, some are for free subscribers, and some are for paid subscribers. The calendar shows the status, but access itself is handled through Substack when you open the lesson.

## View in browser
**[View the course calendar](https://robertmccallnz.github.io/kiwidialecticcalendar-/github-calendar-connector.html)**

## Subscribe on your phone or laptop
**[Subscribe via iCal / ICS](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/robertmccallnz/kiwidialecticcalendar-/main/the-kiwi-dialectic-courses.ics)**

## Access labels
- **Public** = open to anyone.
- **Free subscriber** = you need a free subscription to read the lesson.
- **Paid** = you need a paid subscription to unlock the full lesson.

## Google Calendar
1. Open Google Calendar on desktop.
2. Click the plus sign next to “Other calendars.”
3. Choose **From URL**.
4. Paste the iCal / ICS link.
5. Click **Add calendar**.

## Apple Calendar
1. Open Calendar.
2. Choose **New Calendar Subscription**.
3. Paste the iCal / ICS link.
4. Save.

## Outlook
1. Open Outlook Calendar.
2. Choose **Add calendar**.
3. Select **Subscribe from web**.
4. Paste the iCal / ICS link.
5. Import or subscribe.
