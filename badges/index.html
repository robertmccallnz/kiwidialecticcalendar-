POU TOHU · ACCREDITATION

A pou tohu — post, marker, badge — is awarded after completing each course. Free to use; please credit The Kiwi Dialectic. Every course carries its own motif and colour, drawn from the six kaupapa Māori motifs at the heart of the mahi. Every badge ships in te reo Māori and English; more locales on request.

ACCESSEvery course is free to read. Free subscribers to The Kiwi Dialectic also get chat threads, live Q&A, and course announcements.
