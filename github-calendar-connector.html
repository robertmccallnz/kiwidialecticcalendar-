A public, kaupapa Māori–branded calendar for The Kiwi Dialectic. HTML courses hosted on GitHub Pages, free to read and free to remix under CC BY-SA 4.0. Subscribe on Substack for chat threads, pou tohu, and workbooks.

Kaupapa Māori motif branding Each course carries a motif and colour that reflects its whakaaro — niho taniwha (teeth at the threshold), unaunahi (scales of mutual aid), kōwhaiwhai (painted rafters of shared meaning), koru (unfurling growth), tākarangi (double spiral), and pā tūwatawata (the fortified home course).
